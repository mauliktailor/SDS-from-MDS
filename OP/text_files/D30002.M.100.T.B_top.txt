Honduras braced as category 5 Hurricane Mitch approached.
Slow-moving Mitch battered the Honduran coast for 3 days.
Honduran death estimates grew from 32 to 231 in the first days, to 6,076, with 4,621 missing.
About 2,000 were killed in Nicaragua, 239 in El Salvador, 194 in Guatemala, 6 in southern Mexico and 7 in Costa Rica.
The EU approved 6.4 million ecu in aid to Mitch's victims.
