Tornadoes can occur in any month, but are more frequent fromApril through June and between 3 and 6 p.m. _
There were 32 tornado-related deaths reported in 1988, downfrom 59 in 1987 and well below the average of 99 a year.
_Less than 2 percent of all tornadoes are classified as violent,with wind speeds of more than 200 mph and a path averaging 26miles.
The longest tornado on record went 219 miles acrossMissouri, Illinois and Indiana in March 1925.
Tornadoes travel at an average 30 mph, but can stand still orgo 70 mph.
