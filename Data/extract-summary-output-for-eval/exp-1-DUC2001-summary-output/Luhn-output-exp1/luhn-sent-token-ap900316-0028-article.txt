Tuberculosis was down 8.7 percent among whites in 1988, ascompared with 1985, but up 9.1 percent among blacks and 17.6percent among Hispanics _ two groups with proportionately higherrates of AIDS cases.
In 1988, 7,720 new tuberculosis cases were reported in whites,compared with 8,280 in blacks and 3,637 in Hispanics.
