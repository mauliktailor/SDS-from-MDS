On Monday, two pilots were killed in the crash of an Air ForceF-4 Phantom reconnaissance jet in Saudi Arabia, and just hoursearlier two Marine Corps UH-1 Huey helicopters, each carrying fourcrew members, crashed over the Arabian Sea, killing all eight men.
Five U.S. aircraft have been involved in fatal crashes in thegulf area since the start of Desert Shield two months ago: anF-111, an F-4 Phantom reconnaissance jet, an F-15E and two MarineCorps UH-1 Huey helicopters.
