Introduction: The calm waters of Prince William Soundhave stymied efforts to disperse the largest oil spill in U.S.history, which spewed from a ship that ran aground trying to avoidchunks of ice, officials said.
The Exxon Valdez had loaded 1.2 million barrels of oil at theAlyeska Pipeline Service Co. marine terminal at Valdez and was enroute to Long Beach, Calif., when it crunched onto the reef.
